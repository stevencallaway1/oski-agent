# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other people
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, public or private intimidation, and publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope and Enforcement

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and whenever an individual is officially representing the project. Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers through the repository's private security contact. Maintainers will review and respond to reports as appropriate.
